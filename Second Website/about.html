Why?

This system was created by students for students.
Also for reviewers and supervisors.

Nowadays every student has to do everything by himself. Asking professors for supervision, suggesting the topic of thesis.
Also supervisors have a lot of work to do. Receiving emails, answering to students requests. It can generate a lot of chaos and disorganization.
The best answer for all of this problems is our system, which will help with the whole process. The user can find here all necessary things needed in dissertation process.
Students are able to :

  • choose the topic of their thesis from list of proposed topics
  • upload thesis
  • get feedback from supervisor
  • check progress of grading etc.
Supervisor and reviewers are able to:
  • Check and grade the thesis
  • Post some comments about thesis

We decided to serve everything in the simplest form with easy to understand interface.
