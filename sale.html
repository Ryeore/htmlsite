Sale of games at the Microsoft Store. Available discounts for over 600 titles

July 19 In Microsoft's Store, the sale of games for Xbox One, Xbox 360 and PCs was launched. As part of Xbox Ultimate Game Sale you can find a lot of interesting titles. The list of games is enormous, because there are a total of over 680 items on it (including 566 are console games). It is worth mentioning that some games have been discounted by up to 65 percent. Xbox Live Gold members receive an additional 10% discount.
